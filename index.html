October 3rd – 15th, 2025 · Suleja, Abuja

Obiorah Égoamaka

Works primarily with charcoal and oil paint, focusing on the human form as structure rather than likeness. His practice explores gesture, posture, and physical presence, allowing each figure to exist without performance or exaggeration.

This body of work forms part of an ongoing exhibition, with selected originals available for collection.
