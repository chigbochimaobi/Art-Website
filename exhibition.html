A Contemporary Art Exhibition

Between Presence & Absence

A solo exhibition by Obiorah Egoamaka exploring the fragile tension between the human body, memory, and quiet psychological space.

Date

June 13 to July 3, 2026

Venue

Above Average Arts Gallery

Location

Abuja, Nigeria

About the Exhibition

This exhibition brings together charcoal drawings and paintings that examine the human form as both subject and surface. Through restrained gestures and muted tones, the works invite viewers into moments of stillness, vulnerability, and reflection.



Detailed Directions

The exhibition will take place in Suleja, on the outskirts of Abuja. Visitors coming from Abuja city can access the venue via Maitama Road.
